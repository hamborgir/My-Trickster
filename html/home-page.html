GAMEPLAY

"My TricksTeR" is an immersive pixel art RPG that combines classic role-playing elements with a unique twist on magic and trickery. Set in a vibrant fantasy world, players take on the role of a young apprentice magician who discovers a mysterious ancient book known as the "TricksTeR Tome." This enchanted book holds the secrets to a forgotten art of magical trickery that grants the wielder incredible powers.
